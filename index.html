About Me

I'm an undergraduate Statistics and Neuroscience student at the University of Washington. My work spans computational neuroscience, machine learning, global health analytics, and data engineering. I study neural coherence and cognitive processes in nonhuman primates, build ML pipelines for biomedical data, and design dashboards and modeling systems for public health datasets.

I enjoy turning messy data into structured insights, exploring uncertainty, and building tools that help others understand complex systems.

Research

My research spans computational neuroscience and biomedical ML.
