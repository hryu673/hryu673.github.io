Research Threads

I am drawn to questions where behavior, uncertainty, and biology overlap. Recently, that has meant looking at how pharmacological manipulations reshape neural coherence during texture guided licking, and how survival models can clarify risk dynamics in oncology.

I enjoy moving between experiment design, data cleaning, modeling, and interpretation, keeping an eye on how assumptions shape the stories we tell with our results.
